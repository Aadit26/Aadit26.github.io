COVID HOSPITALS IN MAHARASHTRA .

There are a total of 30 "Covid Hospitals" dedicated in maharashtra . All hospitals are taking every step to cure the patient . All the hospitals have well qualified doctors who daily go there for treating the patients and putting theit lives on risk . The hospital take all the safety measures they are able too .

There are several precautions taken by the hospitals for not leeting the virus exist in that particular hospital .
Some of the precautions are as follow :

1) They are sanitizing the hospital every 6 hours .
2) Every doctor wears a "PPE" kit so that the doctors doesn't catches any infection and the doctors infection is not passed to any other .
3) Socail - Distancing is ment to be compulsory in every hospital .
